Lafayette Instrument Co.

For my senior capstone project at Purdue, I worked with 3 other students to create a web application for Lafayette Instrument Company, the world's leading manufacturer of polygraph equipment*. We created a responsive digital reference application that allows examiners to access several user guides for the polygraph examination process, including searching for keywords. We performed 3 rounds of UX testing with polygraph examiners of various ages, backgrounds, and levels of experience, and ended up with a product well suited for users of all levels of internet proficiency.
